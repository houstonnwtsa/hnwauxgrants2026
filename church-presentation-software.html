Project summary

The Salvation Army Houston Northwest Corps uses presentation software every week for Sunday worship and ministry gatherings. Proclaim helps the corps provide clear, consistent, and professional worship visuals for people in the room and for those connecting through livestream and recorded services.

How this helps the ministry

Professional worship visuals

Supports readable scripture, sermon points, lyrics, announcements, and media backgrounds during worship services.

Online worship support

Helps keep the livestream and recorded services visually clear for homebound members, guests, and people connecting from a distance.

Consistent weekly workflow

Maintains a reliable platform for preparing and presenting weekly worship content without rebuilding the system from scratch.

Grant form wording

Item/Project Requested: Church Presentation Software

Description: Purchase the annual Proclaim worship presentation software and media license for The Salvation Army Houston Northwest Corps. This software is used each week to create and present worship slides, scripture readings, song lyrics, sermon visuals, announcements, and livestream-friendly media. The license allows the corps to continue offering professional, clear, and consistent worship presentations for those attending in person and for the more than 200 people who connect with the worship service online.
