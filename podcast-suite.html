Project summary

The corps already has an active online teaching ministry through the Bible Together video podcast. This request moves the ministry from borrowed, personally owned, and pieced-together equipment toward a reliable corps-owned setup that can be used for Bible teaching, interviews, youth-led content, and media training through the Red Shield Youth Center and church youth programming.

How this helps the ministry

Builds on existing ministry

Bible Together is already being used as an online discipleship tool; the new setup strengthens that proven ministry instead of starting from scratch.

Equips youth with creative skills

Students can learn planning, interviewing, recording, camera framing, editing preparation, and responsible Christian communication.

Expandable for future growth

The setup begins with two microphones and two cameras, while supporting future expansion with existing hardware and additional inputs.

Reference ministry

The Bible Together playlist provides a real example of the corps’ current online teaching ministry and demonstrates how better audio, camera, and production tools can strengthen digital discipleship.

Grant form wording

Item/Project Requested: Podcast Suite / Youth Media Ministry Equipment

Description: Purchase podcast and video recording equipment for the Houston Northwest Corps to strengthen the existing Bible Together online ministry and create a hands-on media training opportunity for youth from the Red Shield Youth Center and church youth programs. Many youth have expressed interest in content creation, and this setup would allow them to learn how to plan, record, film, and produce podcasts and digital ministry content in a supervised Christian environment. The equipment includes a two-camera setup and podcast production tools that can handle two microphones now while remaining expandable with existing hardware for future ministry growth.

Prepared for The Salvation Army Women’s Auxiliary Grant Request 2026.
